Biography

I’m a 1st year Ph.D. student at the University of Central Florida advised by Prof. Yogesh Singh Rawat. I am broadly interested in Computer Vision problems and their applications, in particular how we can utilize unlabeled data and use them for several downstream computer vision tasks such as video classification, segmentation and object detection. I graduated from DTU, India in 2019 with a bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering.
